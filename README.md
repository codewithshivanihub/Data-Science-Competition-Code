# Data-Science-Competition-Code
Data analysis on water shortages in local creeks, specifically Alamo Creek in Dublin, CA.
We used python libraries such as pandas, seaborn, and matplotlib to understand the Alamo Creek water levels and flow rates from 2022 to the present. The creek is home to many plants and animals, and based on the changes in the water levels, action needs to be taken to slow down the rapid decrease in water levels and protect the wildlife.
